JUR.NAL is a semestral magazine released by NOVA Law School's Student's Union. The magazine publishes articles, poems, and other literature from the school's student's.
The relationship between text and its illustration is hard to balance. Unless these images are carefully curated taking into account the intentions of the author, they can change or hinder the tone and intentions of the writer. To avoid this phenomenom we decided to take an abstract aproach, giving the pages an eye-catching characteristic to them, but not so much that it completely deviates the attention from the contents of the writing. These illustrations allow some questions to be raised, engaging the reader on a level that isn't superimposed on the text, but paralel to it.
